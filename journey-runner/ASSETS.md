# Assets

**Art direction:** Chinese mythology style (西游记), vibrant color palette — gold, red, jade green, imperial purple, sky blue. 2D side-scrolling perspective with multi-layer parallax. Characters and objects rendered as clean digital sprites with Chinese mythological aesthetic. Clouds are golden-glowing pathways. Overall tone: epic but playful.
